PLAINTEXT PANIC

A Retro-Styled Word Puzzle Game for macOS

+-----------------------------------------------------------------+ | GET THE HIGHEST SCORE YOU CAN. THEN, TRY TO BEAT THAT RECORD. | +-----------------------------------------------------------------+

ABOUT THE GAME

Plaintext Panic is a fast-paced word puzzle game with Apple II aesthetics. Each round gives you seven letters and two minutes to find as many words as you can. Find the 7-letter Bingos!

FEATURES

  • CRT monitor effects
  • Uses the official NASPA tournament word list (NWL 2023)
  • Two word list options: Full NASPA (25,473 seven-letter words) or Common 7-Letter Words (5,060 recognizable words)
  • Time bonuses for longer words
  • View definitions for all possible words after each round
